How we keep loads moving

Every validation event from this portal flows into NexAI, a platform of autonomous AI agents that triage, fix, and document production incidents in minutes. Carrier lookup failures, PO linkage errors, and weight-check regressions are caught and resolved before they impact your shipments.

  • 1Detect — Grafana watches every validation gate with sub-second granularity. A spike in ERP_PO_LINKAGE_FAILED fires a Sev2 within 30 seconds.
  • 2Triage — NexAI SRE correlates the metric spike to the suspect validator change in under 90 seconds.
  • 3Fix — NexAI Fullstack opens a code-review-ready PR. NexAI SDET adds the regression test that would have caught it.
  • 4Audit — Immutable postmortem written. Human reviews and merges before any change ships.
